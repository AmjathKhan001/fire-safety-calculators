Voltage Drop Calculator (2-Wire DC)

Calculates the voltage drop and End-of-Line (EOL) voltage for a fire alarm circuit based on wire gauge, length, and current draw.

Circuit Parameters

Wire Specifications

Resistivity constant K = 10.4 CM-Ohms/ft (copper).

NEC Requirements

  • Maximum voltage drop: 20% of source voltage
  • Typical requirement: 10-15% maximum drop
  • Use actual measured resistance for critical circuits
  • Consider temperature effects on resistance
  • Include future expansion in calculations
