Safety Precautions

  • CO₂ is an asphyxiant - ensure proper room evacuation
  • Minimum design concentration: 34% for surface fires
  • For deep-seated fires: 65% concentration required
  • Always include safety factors for leakage and temperature
  • Consult NFPA 12 for complete design requirements
