NFPA 72 Requirements

  • Minimum standby time: 24 hours for most systems
  • Minimum alarm time: 5 minutes (non-voice), 15 minutes (voice)
  • Batteries must be sized for 1.25 times calculated load
  • Temperature de-rating required for extreme environments
  • Battery voltage must be within panel operating range
