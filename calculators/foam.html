NFPA 11 Guidelines

  • For Class B hydrocarbon fires: 0.16 GPM/Sq Ft minimum
  • For Class B polar solvent fires: 0.20 GPM/Sq Ft minimum
  • Minimum discharge time: 10 minutes for subsurface, 30 minutes for surface
  • Always verify with local authority having jurisdiction
