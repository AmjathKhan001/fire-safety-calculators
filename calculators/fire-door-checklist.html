Fire Door Inspection Checklist (NFPA 80)

A simplified checklist for inspecting the core operational components of a fire door assembly (NFPA 80, 5.2.4).

NFPA 80 Inspection Notes

  • Annual inspection required for all fire doors
  • Repair or replace failed components immediately
  • Document all inspections and repairs
  • Consult NFPA 80 for complete requirements
  • Maintain inspection records for authorities
