NFPA 1142 Notes

  • Maximum flow limited to 12,000 GPM
  • Minimum flow is 250 GPM
  • For buildings over 3 stories, add 25% per additional story
  • Exposure protection may require additional flow
  • Duration: 2-4 hours depending on construction
