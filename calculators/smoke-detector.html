NFPA 72 Requirements

  • Smooth ceilings ≤ 10 ft: 30 ft maximum spacing
  • For heights > 10 ft: Reduce spacing per NFPA 72 Table 17.5.3.2.1
  • Wall to detector: ½ of calculated spacing
  • Beamed ceilings: Special spacing requirements apply
  • Consider air movement and stratification
