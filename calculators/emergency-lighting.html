NFPA 101 Requirements

  • Minimum illuminance: 1.0 foot-candle average
  • Maximum to minimum ratio: 40:1 maximum
  • Duration: 90 minutes minimum
  • Path markings: Required for steps and changes in level
  • Exit signs: Required at all exits
