Fire Damper / Smoke Damper Selection Guide

Provides guidance on the required type and rating of dampers based on barrier type and HVAC system operation (NFPA 90A/IBC).

Code References

  • NFPA 90A: Standard for Air-Conditioning Systems
  • IBC Chapter 7: Fire and Smoke Protection Features
  • Fire dampers: Tested to UL 555
  • Smoke dampers: Tested to UL 555S
  • Combination dampers: UL 555/UL 555S
